## NMC License Verification Form

```html
CREATE TABLE doctor_verification (
  id INT PRIMARY KEY,
  nmc_license_number VARCHAR(50) UNIQUE,
  full_name VARCHAR(100),
  date_of_birth DATE,
  specialization VARCHAR(50),  -- Dermatology, etc.
  registration_year INT,
  qualification TEXT,          -- MBBS, MD, etc.
  hospital_affiliation VARCHAR(200),
  experience_years INT,
  phone VARCHAR(20),
  email VARCHAR(100),
  verification_status ENUM('pending', 'verified', 'rejected'),
  verified_at DATETIME,
  verified_by VARCHAR(100),    -- Admin who verified
  notes TEXT,
  created_at DATETIME,
  updated_at DATETIME
);
```

### Verification Checklist
- [ ] NMC License Valid
- [ ] License Number Matches NMC Database
- [ ] Active Medical Practice
- [ ] Dermatology Specialization
- [ ] No Complaints Against Doctor
- [ ] Professional Background Check

## FAQ for Doctors

### Income & Payments
**Q: How much can I earn?**
A: It depends on your consultation volume. At 20 consultations/month, you'd earn NPR 3,500-4,000. At 100/month, NPR 17,500-20,000+.

**Q: How do I get paid?**
A: We transfer earnings weekly or monthly via bank transfer or Khalti. Zero fees.

**Q: Are there any charges?**
A: No registration fee. No monthly fee. We only take a commission on consultations and product sales.

### Patients & Schedule
**Q: Can I reject patients?**
A: Yes, you can decline any consultation request without penalty.

**Q: Can I set my own schedule?**
A: Yes, set availability whenever you want. Update anytime.

**Q: What if I'm busy?**
A: Take time off. No pressure to accept consultations during your break.

### Compliance & Verification
**Q: Do you verify NMC licenses?**
A: Yes, we verify all licenses with NMC database before publishing your profile.

**Q: Is patient data secure?**
A: Yes, we follow Nepal's data protection guidelines and medical privacy standards.

**Q: Can I lose my verification?**
A: Only if you violate our code of conduct (unprofessional behavior, incorrect diagnosis, etc.).
